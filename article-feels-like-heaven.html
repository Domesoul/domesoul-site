Release Notes: Feels Like Heaven With You

Published Mar 2025 • 3 min read

If Where the Light Lives is about resilience, Feels Like Heaven With You is about joy — the kind that makes your soul exhale. This song was written in one sitting, late at night, with just a guitar, a dim lamp, and the feeling of being fully present with someone you love.

The Story

Love songs are everywhere, but this one came from a very personal place. It’s about finding that person who feels like “home,” not because they fix you, but because they see you. Whether it’s a friend, partner, or the community you belong to, they become the safe place where you can simply be.

Production

The track blends warm acoustic soul with soft pop textures. I wanted the verses to feel like walking hand-in-hand down a quiet street, and the chorus to feel like stepping into the sunlight together. Layered harmonies and gentle percussion give it that slow-bloom warmth.
