Domé SOUL Bio

Domé Soul is more than a name it’s a voice, a mission, and a movement. Blending Afro-soul, folk, gospel, and conscious pop, his music is rooted in love, service, and storytelling. As a seasoned humanitarian, Domé Soul transforms global struggles into soul-stirring anthems of healing and hope. With powerful duets, worshipful ballads, and uplifting grooves, his debut album “Light in the Dust” carries the listener through loss, faith, brotherhood, and resilience. Whether singing for the voiceless or celebrating divine light, Domé Soul invites you to journey with him where brokenness becomes beauty. Fun Facts: Domé Soul has over 15 years of experience as a humanitarian aid worker. He blends Ibibio, folk, gospel, and acoustic African influences. His first single “Where the Light Lives” features the soulful vocals of Daniella Dee (ElleDee)..
