Where the Light Lives

Behind the Song

This song was born from a quiet question: What if hope had a home?

“Where the Light Lives” was written during a season of shadows — not just in the world, but within. I had seen people laugh with broken hearts, give with empty hands, and walk through fire still singing. And I realized: the light doesn’t always live in perfect places.

It lives in the eyes of a child who still dreams.
In the hands of a stranger who reaches out.
In the quiet strength of those who have every reason to quit — and don’t.

This song is my offering to them.
A reminder that no matter the dust, despair, or distance —
the light still lives. And maybe, just maybe, it lives in us.
