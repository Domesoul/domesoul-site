Feels Like Heaven With You

Behind the Song

Some people don’t just walk into your life — they light it up.

“Feels Like Heaven With You” was written as a gentle celebration of that rare kind of love — the kind that doesn’t just excite you, but anchors you.

It’s about peace that follows presence.
About feeling like home in someone’s laugh, in their silence, in their flaws.

The world can be loud, cruel, uncertain.
But when I’m with you — everything softens.

In that stillness, in that knowing…
it feels like heaven.
