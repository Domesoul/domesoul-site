About Domé Soul

Domé Soul is the musical voice of a humanitarian worker, blending Afro-soul, gospel, and storytelling into heartfelt tracks. With over 15 years of service across countries like Yemen, South Sudan, Malawi, Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Uganda, Kenya, Chad, Liberia, and Niger, Domé brings real-world compassion into every lyric.

The music speaks for the unheard — from dusty camps to hopeful hearts — sharing light, love, and resilience through melody and message.
