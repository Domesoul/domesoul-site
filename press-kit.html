Artist Bio

Domé Soul is an Afro-soul and humanitarian-inspired artist whose music blends soul, gospel, and folk traditions with powerful storytelling. With tracks like "Where the Light Lives" and "Feels Like Heaven With You", Domé Soul aims to inspire light, healing, and mission in every listener's heart.
