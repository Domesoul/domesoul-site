Artist Bio

Short Bio

Domé Soul is a Nigerian Afro-soul artist blending soul, gospel, and folk into heartfelt music that speaks of healing, faith, and community. With over 15 years of humanitarian work across crisis zones worldwide, he uses his platform to inspire compassion through both his songs and his outreach initiative, Soul Mission. Known for emotive tracks like Where the Light Lives and Feels Like Heaven With You, Domé Soul bridges the worlds of art and humanitarianism—creating music with purpose, hope, and connection.

Full Bio

Domé Soul is an Afro-soul artist whose work blends the intimacy of soul, the reverence of gospel, and the storytelling of folk. His songs explore healing, faith, community, and the light we carry for one another—music that feels personal yet communal, crafted to restore and to steady.

Born and raised in Nigeria and shaped by global experiences, Domé has spent more than fifteen years working in humanitarian and development spaces, coordinating field projects and advocacy across crisis-affected regions. Those years inform his songs: they are compassionate, clear-eyed, and purpose-driven. Through his outreach initiative Soul Mission, he connects music with tangible support—spotlighting need, inviting generosity, and telling stories that dignify people and places.

Recent releases include the contemplative Where the Light Lives—a meditation on courage and quiet hope—and Feels Like Heaven With You, a warm, devotional love song about finding rest in relationship and in God’s nearness. Upcoming projects expand his acoustic palette with hand-played textures, choir layers, and folk percussion—keeping the songs human, steady, and soul-anchored.

Domé Soul is building a body of work that lives beyond the stream: songs to carry into ordinary days, and an ethos that pairs art with responsibility. It’s music you can feel—and a mission you can join.

  • Genres: Afro-soul, gospel, folk
  • Themes: healing, faith, community, compassion, hope
  • Initiative: Soul Mission — music + humanitarian outreach
  • Key tracks: “Where the Light Lives”, “Feels Like Heaven With You”
