Artist Bio

Domé Soul is an Afro-folk & gospel project blending warm acoustic textures with honest storytelling. Rooted in the belief that “where the light lives, healing begins,” the music carries a mission beyond stages — supporting real community outreach through the Soul Mission initiative.

Recent singles include “Where the Light Lives,” “Feels Like Heaven With You,” and “Still I Will Love You.” Domé Soul has been featured on local radio and in community events where songs and service meet.
